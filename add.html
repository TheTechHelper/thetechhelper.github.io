Add to home screen
On Android: Go to the homepage, press the three dots in the top-right of the browser, and click on 'Add to home screen'
On Safari: Click the Share icon and click 'Add to home screen'
Add to desktop
On Chrome, go to the homepage, click on the three dots at the top-right, hover over 'More Tools' then click on 'Create shortcut...'. Make sure that 'Open as window' is checked and click 'Create'.
Add to taskbar on Windows
Follow the steps above in 'Add to desktop', then open the app, right-click on it in the taskbar, and click 'Pin to taskbar'.
